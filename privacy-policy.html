1. Introduction

Palladium Works ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and safety of our players. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights players have when using our Roblox games and community platforms.

By using our services, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly from players. However, we may receive certain data from:

A. Roblox Platform

Roblox automatically provides:

  • Username
  • User ID
  • Avatar appearance
  • Game statistics (playtime, progress, items, badges)
  • Device type (PC/mobile/console)

B. Game Systems

Inside our experiences, we may collect:

  • Progress data (levels, currency, tools)
  • Gameplay actions
  • Moderation logs (warnings, kicks, bans)
  • Bug reports or system errors

C. Third-Party Platforms

If you join optional community platforms such as Discord (13+ only), those platforms may collect their own information.

3. How We Use Information

  • Operate and maintain our games
  • Save player data and progress
  • Improve game performance and stability
  • Detect and prevent exploiting or rule-breaking
  • Communicate updates or responses to inquiries
