1. Introduction

Palladium Works ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and safety of our players. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights players have when using our Roblox games and community platforms. By using our services, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly from players. However, we may receive certain data from:

A. Roblox Platform

Roblox automatically provides:

  • Username
  • User ID
  • Avatar appearance
  • Game statistics (playtime, progress, items, badges)
  • Device type (PC/mobile/console)

B. Game Systems

Inside our experiences, we may collect:

  • Progress data (levels, currency, tools)
  • Gameplay actions
  • Moderation logs (warnings, kicks, bans)
  • Bug reports or system errors

C. Third-Party Platforms

If you join optional community platforms such as Discord (13+ only), those platforms may collect their own information. We do not control external platform privacy settings.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate and maintain our games
  • Save player data and progress
  • Improve game performance and stability
  • Detect and prevent exploiting or rule violations
  • Provide customer support
  • Keep our community safe

We never sell or share user data for commercial purposes.

4. Data Sharing

We may share relevant information only when necessary:

  • With Roblox: if required for moderation or safety
  • With platform services: to operate game features
  • With law enforcement: if required by law or to protect player safety

We do not sell, trade, or rent player information.

5. Data Storage

Player data is stored using Roblox DataStores, or other Roblox-provided systems. We cannot guarantee permanent storage because:

  • Roblox may experience outages
  • Data resets may occur during development
  • Corrupted data may be automatically cleared

6. User Rights

Players have the right to:

  • Request clarification about what data we store.
  • Request deletion of in-game data (if technically possible)
  • Appeal moderation actions through our Appeals Policy.

We cannot modify or recover Roblox account information.

7. Safety of Minors

Players under 13 may play our Roblox experiences. They may not join age-restricted platforms such as Discord. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors outside of what Roblox automatically provides.

8. Third-Party Links

Our community platforms (e.g., Discord) have their own Privacy Policies. We are not responsible for data collected by third-party services.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of our services means you accept the updated version.
